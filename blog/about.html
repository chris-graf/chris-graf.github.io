About the Author

Chris Graf

This DC-raised, Chicago-resident student is eager to advance his programming education at Dev Bootcamp. I've been through many career-goal phases in my short 24 years, but I've got a strong sense that this is the one that will stick.
My most recent hobbies include sketch writing, improv, and attending shows showcasing the aforementioned art forms. In 7th grade I played a tree in the Wizard of Oz, inflaming my abiding love for theatre in all its forms.
I graduated from the University of Chicago with the Class of 2013 with a bachelor's in Sociology. I'm also pretty sure I did a minor in Spanish Language and Literature, because I completed all the clases and submitted the forms, but no one ever actually confirmed that in word or writing. This one is on you, University of Chicago Registrar's Office. Que asco.
